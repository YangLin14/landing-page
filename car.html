Arduino Joystick Control Car

A control car designed on Arduino Nano and many other components.

Summary

As a vice president of Ohlone IEEE club, I led the club to designed and created an Arduino joystick control car that moves in different directions based on the joystick position. We implemented 3D printing to create the car's body and controller. Programmed in Arduino and completed the assembly of the car. Finally we showcased the car to the school and the students during the career fair.
