Rube-Goldberg Machine

A Rube-Goldberg machine designed on Arduino.

Summary

Got in touch in 2021 with Arduino and wanted to create something with it. This project is the result of that. This project was assigned to me in an online camp at Engineering Possibilities in College (EPIC) from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo.

Gotten familiar in circuits assembly by creating different small hands-on projects. And finally, created a Rube-Goldberg machine with the combination of everything I've learned and used.
