Week 1 in Headstarter

The first week of Headstarter.

Summary

I started off the first week of Headstarter by learning the basics of HTML and CSS to create my first personal website. Pulling inspiration from various websites and online tutorials, I am able to continue to improve and add new features to it.

In addition to attending daily meetings where I listened to various startup co-founders discuss their journeys, I gained valuable insights into different tech fields and how to navigate various career paths. I learned that success isn't just about writing code; it's also about effectively marketing ourselves. The products we create have little value if they aren't reaching and benefiting clients.

Moreover, there are mock interviews for us to practice and get used to.
